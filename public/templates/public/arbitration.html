Arbitration

Welcome to the arbitration page. If you have a dispute that you would like to have resolved through arbitration, please review the following information carefully.

Site Policy

Before submitting a request for arbitration, please review our site policy to ensure that your dispute falls within the scope of issues that can be resolved through this process.

Submitting a Request

To submit a request for arbitration, please follow the appropriate format and provide all necessary information as outlined in our site policy. Failure to do so may result in your request being denied.

Responding to a Request

If you have received a request for arbitration, please review it carefully and follow the appropriate format for responding. Be sure to provide all necessary information and any relevant supporting documentation.

Judging the Merits of a Dispute

Our arbitration panel will carefully review all information provided by both parties and make a determination based on the merits of the case. In making this determination, we will consider whether either party has violated any provisions outlined in our site policy.
